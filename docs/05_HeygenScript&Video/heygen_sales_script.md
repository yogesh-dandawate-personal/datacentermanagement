## SPEAKER NOTES & VISUAL GUIDANCE

### **Production Tips for HeyGen:**

1. **Avatar Style**: Professional, confident presenter (recommend Midjourney-style realistic avatar or polished AI presenter)
2. **Pacing**: Conversational, slightly faster (120-130 wpm) to fit 2:30 runtime
3. **Tone**: Expert, solution-focused, not fear-based
4. **Visuals**:
   - Use actual iNetZero mockups/screenshots where available
   - Data center imagery for context
   - Animated charts showing energy/carbon flows
   - Workflow diagrams (maker-checker-reviewer)
   - Split-screen comparisons (before/after)

### **Background Assets Needed:**

- Modern data center photography
- iNetZero dashboard screenshots
- Energy consumption charts
- Carbon metrics visualizations
- Approval workflow diagrams
- Testimonial clips (optional)

### **Music & Sound:**

- Upbeat but professional background music (70-80 dB)
- Sound effects for transitions (subtle whooshes)
- Clear audio with no clipping

### **Call-to-Action Variations:**

- **B2B Focus**: "Schedule a 15-minute demo"
- **Enterprise Focus**: "Contact our solution architect"
- **Self-Service**: "Start your free trial at icarbon.io"
