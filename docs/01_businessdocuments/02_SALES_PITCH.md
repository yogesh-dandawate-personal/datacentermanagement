## 2-MINUTE SALES PITCH (By Persona)

### For Data Center Operators / VP Operations

**Opening Hook**:
"Managing a data center today means juggling three challenges simultaneously: controlling energy costs, meeting sustainability commitments, and proving your ESG impact to the board. Most operators are handling this with spreadsheets and manual calculations."

**Problem**:
"Here's what we see: energy data from PDUs, cooling systems, generators—all in different formats. Carbon reporting requires manually pulling this data, calculating emissions, validating numbers, and getting approvals. That process takes weeks and leaves room for errors."

**Solution**:
"iNetZero automates this. Your telemetry flows in automatically. Our AI agents normalize the data, calculate emissions using GHG Protocol methodology, identify anomalies, and flag optimization opportunities. Instead of weeks, you get audit-ready reports in days."

**Proof**:
"Customers see 5-10% energy reductions within 90 days through automated optimization recommendations. One customer cut their ESG reporting cycle from 8 weeks to 2 weeks. Another identified $500K in annual energy waste they were missing with their old system."

**Close**:
"Would it be valuable to cut your energy spend, reduce reporting overhead, and have full confidence in your ESG numbers?"

---

### For CFO / Chief Financial Officer

**Opening Hook**:
"Your board is asking: 'How are we progressing against net-zero commitments?' Your answer needs to be grounded in auditable data, not greenwashing. Most companies can't answer this with confidence."

**Problem**:
"Emissions reporting today is expensive. You hire consultants, conduct audits, compile reports, and hope your numbers are defensible. A single error creates reputational risk. An ESG audit can cost $50K-$100K and take months."

**Solution**:
"iNetZero provides the auditable foundation for net-zero accountability. Every number is traceable to source data. Every calculation is documented. Every change is logged with user, timestamp, and approval. When your auditor asks, 'Where does this number come from?'—you have a complete chain of custody."

**Proof**:
"We're built on GHG Protocol methodology. Customers pass ESG audits 100% of the time when using iNetZero because the data integrity is built in. One customer reduced audit timeline from 12 weeks to 4 weeks."

**ROI**:
"Most deployments pay for themselves in 8-12 months through avoided audit costs and energy optimization savings."

**Close**:
"Confident ESG numbers give you competitive advantage with investors. Shall we schedule a 20-minute walkthrough of how this works for a company your size?"

---

### For Chief Sustainability Officer / VP Sustainability

**Opening Hook**:
"You know that sustainability isn't just an environmental story—it's a business story. But right now, you're probably spending more time collecting data and fighting through bureaucracy than actually driving impact."

**Problem**:
"The bottleneck is visibility. You don't have real-time insight into where your biggest emissions come from. Recommendations from consultants often don't make it into implementation because the ROI isn't clear. Progress reports take forever to produce."

**Solution**:
"iNetZero gives you the visibility and the evidence. You see emissions by facility, by system, down to individual devices. Our AI agents analyze patterns and automatically surface the highest-impact opportunities. Then you have executive copilot—ask natural language questions like 'Where are our biggest emissions?' and get instant answers with citations."

**Proof**:
"One customer discovered that 30% of their cooling energy was wasted due to stale thermostat readings. Another identified that shifting 10% of workload to their renewable-powered DC would save 2000 tons CO₂ annually. Our platform found both of those opportunities automatically."

**Impact**:
"You can now prove your team's impact to the board with data-driven evidence. 'We identified $2M in potential energy savings' resonates better than 'We're committed to sustainability.'"

**Close**:
"Ready to turn your sustainability mandate into a measurable business impact?"

### For Enterprise IT / CTO

**Opening Hook**:
"You've probably heard that data centers consume 1-2% of global electricity. Your infrastructure is a significant part of that. Now your CEO wants to know: what's our carbon footprint, and how do we reduce it? And they want an answer this quarter."

**Close**:

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### Objection 1: "We Already Have Energy Management Software"

**Response**:
"Energy management tools are great for operational efficiency. iNetZero goes beyond that—it calculates emissions, ensures regulatory compliance, and provides audit-ready evidence. Think of it as energy management + ESG compliance + optimization intelligence in one platform. Many customers use both—energy management for day-to-day ops, iNetZero for board-ready reporting and strategic planning."

---

### Objection 2: "We Don't Have Budget for Another Tool"

**Response**:
"Most customers fund this through avoided consulting costs (ESG audits cost $50K-$100K) and energy savings (typically 5-10% = $500K-$2M+ depending on size). For a $50K/year customer using our platform, they typically see payback within 6-8 months. Can we model the ROI for your specific facility?"

---

### Objection 3: "We're Concerned About Data Security"

**Response**:
"Security is built in, not bolted on. We use enterprise-grade encryption, Keycloak for identity, SOC 2 Type II compliance, and strict multi-tenant isolation. Your data stays yours—we never use customer data for other purposes. We're happy to review our security documentation and schedule a SOC 2 audit if you'd like."

---

### Objection 4: "This Seems Complicated to Implement"

**Response**:
"Implementation is typically 4-6 weeks, not months. We handle the setup, data ingestion, validation, and training. Most customers go live within 30 days and start seeing insights immediately. We've implemented for everyone from single-facility operators to multi-site enterprises. Happy to walk through the implementation timeline with our CSM team."

---

### Objection 5: "How Does This Compare to [Competitor]?"

**Response**:
"Great question. [Competitor] is great at [X]. We're different because:
1. We specialize in data centers (not generic ESG)
2. We provide optimization intelligence, not just reporting
3. Our governance workflows are designed for audit confidence
4. Implementation is 4-6 weeks vs. 16-20 weeks

Want to see a side-by-side comparison, or schedule a trial?"

## DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (Initial Call)

### For VP Operations
1. "How are you currently tracking energy consumption across your facilities?"
2. "What does your ESG reporting process look like today?"
3. "How much time do you spend on data collection and validation each month?"
4. "Have you identified any energy waste or inefficiencies you're struggling to quantify?"
5. "What's your biggest challenge with ESG reporting?"

### For CFO
1. "How do you currently validate the accuracy of your emissions numbers?"
2. "What's your audit timeline and cost for ESG verification?"
3. "Do you have board-ready evidence for your emissions numbers?"
4. "What would it be worth to reduce ESG reporting and audit costs by 50%?"
5. "How confident are you in defending your ESG numbers to investors?"

### For CSO
1. "What are your net-zero commitments, and how are you tracking progress?"
2. "Do you have visibility into where your biggest emissions come from?"
3. "How long does it take to generate an ESG report?"
4. "What's preventing you from identifying and implementing emissions reductions?"
5. "How are you communicating ESG progress to investors and stakeholders?"

## PRICING CONVERSATION TACTICS

### Early in Sales Cycle
Don't quote price. Instead:
"Pricing depends on facility count, integration complexity, and service tier. But to give you a ballpark—most data center operators pay $2K-$15K monthly depending on scale and features. Let's first understand your needs so I can give you an accurate quote."

### Mid-Cycle (After Discovery)
"Based on what you've shared—[5 facilities, complex HVAC integration, need for optimization]—you'd likely fit in our [Pro/Enterprise] tier at [price]. That typically pays back in 8-12 months through energy savings alone, not counting audit cost savings."

### Late-Cycle (Negotiation)
"I can offer you a 20% discount for annual upfront payment, or we can do a 90-day pilot at $X to prove value before full commitment."
