## PRICING MODEL

### SaaS Subscription Tiers

#### **Starter Tier**
- **Monthly**: $2,000 | **Annual**: $20,000
- **Facilities**: 1
- **Features**:
  - Basic telemetry ingestion (API, CSV)
  - Energy dashboards (single facility)
  - Monthly reporting
  - Email support
- **Use Case**: Single small data center, pilot deployments
- **Customer Type**: Regional operators, smaller colocation providers

---

#### **Pro Tier**
- **Monthly**: $5,000 | **Annual**: $50,000
- **Facilities**: 5
- **Features**:
  - Advanced telemetry ingestion (API, CSV, BACnet)
  - Energy analytics + trend analysis
  - Carbon calculations (Scope 1 & 2)
  - KPI engine (PUE, CUE, WUE)
  - Approval workflows
  - Quarterly reporting
  - Priority email/chat support
- **Use Case**: Mid-market operator with 2-5 facilities
- **Customer Type**: Enterprise IT, colocation operators

---

#### **Enterprise Tier**
- **Monthly**: $15,000 | **Annual**: $150,000
- **Facilities**: Unlimited
- **Features**:
  - All Pro features
  - Multi-facility hierarchy + cross-facility reporting
  - AI agents (anomaly detection, optimization recommendations)
  - Executive copilot (Q&A with citations)
  - Evidence repository with versioning
  - Maker-checker-reviewer workflows
  - Custom KPI builder
  - Real-time alerting
  - API access (custom integrations)
  - Monthly reporting + custom schedules
  - Dedicated account manager
  - SLA: 99.9% uptime
  - Monthly success reviews
- **Use Case**: Large operators, hyperscalers, enterprise multi-facility
- **Customer Type**: Fortune 500, hyperscalers, major colocation providers

---

#### **Premium Tier**
- **Monthly**: Custom | **Annual**: Custom
- **Facilities**: Unlimited
- **Features**:
  - All Enterprise features
  - Dedicated infrastructure option
  - White-label reporting
  - Custom integrations (BACnet, Modbus, SNMP, proprietary)
  - Custom AI models training
  - 24/7 phone support
  - Quarterly business reviews
  - Custom data modeling
- **Use Case**: Largest customers, strategic partnerships
- **Customer Type**: Hyperscalers (AWS, Google, Meta), major corporations with 50+ facilities

---

### Pricing Rationale

**Per-Facility Model**:
- Scales with value delivered (more facilities = more value)
- Easier to understand and compare
- Reduces negotiation friction

**Industry Benchmarks**:
- Generic ESG SaaS: $50K-$200K annually
- Energy management SaaS: $30K-$150K annually
- Data center DCIM: $50K-$150K annually
- **iNetZero positioning**: $20K-$180K (more affordable than competitors, premium for value)

**Gross Margin Target**:
- Starter: 70% (higher margin, lower CAC)
- Pro: 75% (balanced)
- Enterprise: 80% (scale, lower per-customer cost)
- Premium: 85% (custom, high-touch)
