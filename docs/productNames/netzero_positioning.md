# Net-Zero Positioning & Naming Strategy for iNetZero
**Purpose**: Align iNetZero positioning with enterprise net-zero commitments
**Context**: Data centers are under pressure to meet 2030-2050 net-zero targets
**Date**: March 9, 2026

---

## MARKET CONTEXT: WHY NET-ZERO MATTERS FOR DATA CENTERS

### The Challenge
- **Data center energy** = 1-2% of global electricity (IEA data)
- **Enterprise net-zero commitments**: 90% of Fortune 500 have 2030-2050 targets
- **Scope 2 emissions** from data centers are largest controllable cost driver
- **Investor pressure**: ESG ratings tied to net-zero progress
- **Regulatory pressure**: EU Digital Services Act, carbon border adjustments

### The Opportunity
**Position iNetZero as the platform that enables data centers to achieve net-zero.**
