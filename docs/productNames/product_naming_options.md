# iNetZero Alternative Product Names
**Purpose**: Premium naming options for the agentic ESG platform for data centers
**Date**: March 9, 2026
**Style Guide**: Apple-inspired lowercase "i" prefix + descriptive suffix

---

## TIER 1: PREMIUM "i" BRANDED NAMES (Apple-Style)

### 1. **i-Carbon**
- **Current name** - Solid, direct, recognizable
- **Positioning**: The carbon intelligence platform
- **Tagline**: "Carbon tracking that's intelligent, auditable, trusted."
- **Domain**: icarbon.io
- **Pros**: Short, memorable, ESG-clear
- **Cons**: May feel too technical for C-suite

---

### 2. **i-Sustain**
- **Positioning**: Enterprise sustainability intelligence
- **Tagline**: "Sustain what matters. Track what counts."
- **Domain**: isustain.io
- **Pros**: Aspirational, broader than just carbon
- **Cons**: Generic in crowded ESG market

---

### 3. **i-Impact**
- **Positioning**: Measure and optimize environmental impact
- **Tagline**: "See your impact. Improve your footprint."
- **Domain**: iimpact.io
- **Pros**: Emotional, action-oriented, appeals to purpose-driven orgs
- **Cons**: Trendy, might feel like greenwashing platform

---

### 4. **i-Footprint**
- **Positioning**: Carbon footprint intelligence for enterprises
- **Tagline**: "Know your footprint. Reduce your impact."
- **Domain**: ifootprint.io
- **Pros**: Clear, metaphorical, user-centric
- **Cons**: Slightly informal for enterprise IT

---

### 5. **i-Compass**
- **Positioning**: Navigate your sustainability journey
- **Tagline**: "Your compass for carbon clarity."

### 6. **i-Scope**
