## Sales Conversation Flows

### For Initial Prospect Call (10 minutes)

**Opening** (2 min):
"Thanks for making time. I'm calling because most datacenters we talk to have the same challenge: they have tons of operational data—power consumption, cooling, fuel usage—but no clear picture of their actual carbon impact. Does that sound familiar?"

**Discovery** (3 min):
- Where do you currently track emissions? (Excel, manual, nothing?)
- Who on your team needs this information? (Ops, sustainability, finance?)
- What's your timeline for net-zero or emissions reduction goals?
- What's preventing you from tracking this today?

**Solution** (3 min):
"Here's what we do: We connect to your existing systems—BMS, PDU, cloud infrastructure—and give you real-time visibility into emissions. You get dashboards for operations, compliance reports for your sustainability team, and ROI analysis for finance."

**Close** (2 min):
"I'd love to show you a quick demo tailored to a datacenter like yours. Are you free Thursday at 2 PM for 20 minutes?"

## Discovery Questions Checklist

### Initial Qualification
- [ ] How many facilities do you operate?
- [ ] What's your current emissions baseline?
- [ ] Do you have public net-zero or ESG goals?
- [ ] Who owns sustainability vs. operations vs. finance?
- [ ] What regulatory or investor pressures exist?
- [ ] What's your timeline for action? (6 months? 2 years?)
- [ ] What's your annual spend on sustainability initiatives?

### Technical Discovery
- [ ] What BMS do you use? (BuildingOS, Johnson Controls, etc.?)
- [ ] Do you have PDU or metering data? (How granular?)
- [ ] Are you on cloud? (AWS, Azure, GCP, hybrid?)
- [ ] What's your current emissions tracking method? (Manual? Software?)
- [ ] How often do you need updated metrics? (Real-time? Daily? Monthly?)

### Decision-Maker Discovery
- [ ] Who will use this system day-to-day?
- [ ] Who approves sustainability spending?
- [ ] Who needs sign-off from IT/security?
- [ ] What's the typical procurement process?
- [ ] How quickly can you move? (Weeks? Months?)

## Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Root Cause | Response |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| "We already track this" | Underestimating complexity | "How do you consolidate Scope 1, 2, and 3? Most companies tell us they're doing it manually or incompletely. Let me show you what comprehensive looks like." |
| "This is too expensive" | Don't see ROI | "Most customers find 15-20% efficiency improvements. At your scale, that's $X/year savings. The system pays for itself in month Y." |
| "We don't have buy-in yet" | Stakeholder alignment | "Let's start with operations and sustainability. Once they see the value, the CFO will see the ROI. Can you introduce me to [key stakeholder]?" |
| "Our IT won't approve it" | Security/integration concerns | "We're SOC 2 Type II certified and API-first. Your IT team can review our security docs. Most implementations are API integrations—no major infrastructure changes." |
| "We'll do this later" | Not urgent | "I understand—but ask yourself: Will you reduce your emissions without measuring them? Every month you wait is data you're not collecting." |
| "We'll build it in-house" | Overconfidence | "You could—but our customers would rather focus on their core business. We do one thing: emissions intelligence. It's our expertise." |
