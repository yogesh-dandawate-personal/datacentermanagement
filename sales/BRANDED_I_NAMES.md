## Why "i" Branding?

The "i" prefix strategy (pioneered by Apple) creates:
- ✅ **Instant Recognition**: Think iPhone, iPad, iTunes
- ✅ **Modern Tech Feel**: Implies innovation, intelligence
- ✅ **Personal Connection**: "i" = intelligent, intuitive, innovative
- ✅ **Simple & Memorable**: Single syllable before name
- ✅ **Clean Design**: Works perfectly in logos and marketing
- ✅ **Global Appeal**: Pronounceable in most languages
- ✅ **Defensive Trademark**: Easier to protect and brand

## Marketing & Branding Strategy for "i" Names

### Visual Branding
```
Logo Strategy:
- Lowercase "i" as primary brand mark
- Optional: Icon/symbol integrated with dot
- Examples:
  • iCarbon: i + carbon atom symbol
  • iScope: i + telescope/lens
  • iPulse: i + pulse line
  • iMetrics: i + graph/chart
  • iAlign: i + alignment arrows
```

### Brand Voice
- Modern, intelligent, intuitive
- Simple but sophisticated
- Action-oriented
- Forward-thinking
- Professional yet approachable

### Tagline Formula
**"Intelligent [Action/Noun]"**
- iCarbon: "Intelligent Carbon Management"
- iScope: "Intelligent Scope Management"
- iMetrics: "Intelligent Emissions Metrics"
- iPulse: "Intelligent Emissions Monitoring"
