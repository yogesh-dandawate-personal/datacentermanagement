## 🎯 Recommended Product Name: iCarbon

### The Decision

**PRIMARY CHOICE**: **iCarbon**

**Why**:
- ✅ Most memorable "i" branded option
- ✅ Apple-style premium positioning
- ✅ Clear emissions/carbon focus
- ✅ Broadest market appeal
- ✅ Strongest all-around branding
- ✅ Easy to trademark and defend
- ✅ Scales to product family (iCarbon Suite, Monitor, Reports, Insights)

### Product Identity

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Name** | iCarbon |
| **Tagline** | "Intelligent Carbon Management" |
| **Domain** | icarbon.io |
| **Logo** | Lowercase "i" + carbon atom symbol |
| **Brand Feel** | Premium, modern, intuitive |
| **Positioning** | "Operating system for carbon intelligence" |
| **ACV** | $50K-150K |
| **Target Segments** | All (SMB to enterprise) |

### Key Messaging

**Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)**:
> "iCarbon is the intelligent operating system for emissions management. It automatically tracks your carbon footprint across all operations, gives you real-time visibility, and recommends specific actions to reduce emissions. Think of it like a GPS for getting to net-zero."

**Full Pitch (2 minutes)**:
> "Most datacenters are flying blind when it comes to emissions. They have data scattered across BMS systems, PDUs, and spreadsheets—but no unified view of their carbon impact.
>
> iCarbon changes that. We consolidate all your operational data and transform it into clear, actionable insights. You get real-time visibility, compliance-ready reporting, actionable recommendations, and goal tracking.
>
> Your operations team gets alerts when something's off. Your sustainability team gets the reports they need. Your CFO sees the cost savings. Everyone wins."

## 🎤 Sales Scripts Quick Reference

### Elevator Pitch (30 Seconds)
**Use When**: Initial call, quick introduction

> "iCarbon is the intelligent operating system for emissions management. It automatically tracks your carbon footprint across all operations, gives you real-time visibility, and recommends specific actions to reduce emissions. Think of it like a GPS for getting to net-zero."

### Discovery Opening (2 Minutes)
**Use When**: Starting a discovery call

> "Thanks for making time. I'm calling because most datacenters we talk to have the same challenge: they have tons of operational data—power consumption, cooling, fuel usage—but no clear picture of their actual carbon impact. Does that sound familiar?"

### Value Proposition
**Use When**: Explaining benefits

> "iCarbon consolidates all your operational data and transforms it into clear, actionable insights. You get:
> - Real-time visibility into emissions
> - Compliance-ready reporting (GRI, TCFD, CDP)
> - Actionable recommendations for 15-20% efficiency gains
> - Goal tracking and progress visualization"

### ROI/Financial Close
**Use When**: Finance is in conversation

> "Most of our customers find 15-20% efficiency improvements. At your scale [X tons/month], that's $Y cost savings annually. The system pays for itself in Z months, while also reducing your emissions and getting you compliance-ready."

## 🔍 Discovery Questions Checklist

Use these to qualify and understand prospect needs:

### Initial Qualification (3 minutes)
- [ ] How many facilities do you operate?
- [ ] What's your current emissions baseline?
- [ ] Do you have public net-zero or ESG goals?
- [ ] What regulatory or investor pressures exist?
- [ ] What's your timeline for action?

### Technical Discovery (5 minutes)
- [ ] What BMS/monitoring systems do you use?
- [ ] Do you have PDU or metering data?
- [ ] Are you on cloud? (AWS, Azure, GCP, hybrid?)
- [ ] How often do you need updated metrics?

### Organizational Discovery (5 minutes)
- [ ] Who will use this system day-to-day?
- [ ] Who approves sustainability spending?
- [ ] Who needs IT/security sign-off?
- [ ] How quickly can you move?
