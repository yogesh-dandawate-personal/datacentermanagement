## Executive Summary

This document presents **50 carefully curated product name candidates** for the ESG emissions management platform. Names are organized by strategy and include brand positioning, domain availability considerations, and marketing appeal.

## Naming Strategy

The product name should:
- ✅ Be **memorable and professional** for B2B SaaS
- ✅ **Convey purpose**: emissions/carbon/sustainability focus
- ✅ **Appeal to datacenters**: tech-savvy, metrics-focused audience
- ✅ **Be defensible**: unique, trademark-able, domain-available
- ✅ **Be global**: pronounceable, no negative connotations
- ✅ **Scale**: work for enterprise positioning

## FAQ - Product Naming

**Q: Why not include existing product names?**
A: This is an original product with unique features (Scope 1-3, real-time, ML, SaaS modules) warranting a unique name.

**Q: Should we use the datacenter focus?**
A: Not required - the market is broader (offices, facilities, manufacturing). Generic sustainability positioning is stronger.

**Q: What about competitor names?**
A: Current competitors include Envirotech Consulting, Ecodesk, etc. Our distinctive tech (AI, SaaS, real-time) justifies differentiated naming.

**Q: Can we use a founder's name?**
A: Possible but limits growth story and global appeal. Tech/descriptive names scale better.

**Q: Should .com be required?**
A: No. .io is standard for SaaS, .co acceptable. .com less available but not necessary.
